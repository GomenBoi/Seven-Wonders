Petra Jordan

Welcome to Petra Jordan! This is an archaelogical site which was rumored to be built around 312 BCE and is considered one of the oldest cities in the world.

Country and Origin

Petra is a city found in Jordan which is one of the archaelogical attractions of the world, it originates from the Nabataenese people who built the entire city through ancient means.

Purpose

Petra was a thriving trading center in between 312 BC and 106 AD, where it was the capital of the anciety Nabataen empire.

Design

The architectural style of the Nabataeans was a blend of Assyrian, Egyptian, Hellenistic, and Roman influences, which was integrated into the construction of the ancient city, Petra, building monuments and vast tombs.

Entrance

The entrance to the city of Petra is through the Siq, a narrow gorge, over 1km in length, which is surrounded on both sides by 80m high cliffs.

Royal Tombs

The city of Petra Jordan also hosts many royal tombs which were used in the ancient times to decorate the bodies of rich people.
