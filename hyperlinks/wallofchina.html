The Wall of China

Welcome to the Wall of China! This extravagant monument is a series of fortifications built by the Chinese around 220 BCE, it was made for protection against various nomadic groups that were present in the Eurasian Steppe. -Wikipedia

Country

The countries of which the great wall of China originates are part of northern China such as Qinghai, Gansu, Ningxia and many more.

Purpose

The purpose of the great wall of China was to act as a barrier in the wars between northern China and the Eurasian Steppe so that they could gain a military and strategical advantage through the natural height and length of the wall.

Progenitors and Main Figures

The main people behind the construction of the great wall of China were the Ming Dynasty, as they were famous for building the most renowed parts of the wall. However, there were other kingdoms in play such as the Qin dynasty who also cooperated with the Ming dynasty.

Year of Construction

The great wall of China was constructed around 220 BCE, during the unification of China.

Dimensions

The great wall of China ranges a length of more than 21,000 kilometers across the northern China border.

Details about Construction

The main construction materials used were compromised of mainly stone, brick, lime and wood, however they took advantages of any other resources available to them locally.
